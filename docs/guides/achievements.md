All achievement NFTs are **soulbound**, meaning:

- They permanently represent your accomplishments
- They are minted on Base L2 for low gas costs
- They contribute to your on-chain reputation

## How to Claim Achievements

Achievements are automatically detected and claimed when you meet the requirements:

1. **Complete the requirement** (e.g., finish 10 missions)
2. **Achievement unlocks** - You'll see a notification in the app
3. **NFT mints** - The soulbound NFT is minted to your wallet
4. **XP awarded** - Bonus XP is added to your balance

No manual claiming required!
